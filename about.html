About Chiklee Music

Chiklee Music is a leading force in the Bangladeshi music industry, managing a lot of the market share with a catalog of over 5,000 fresh audio tracks. Our mission is to empower artists and labels by providing world-class music distribution, copyright protection, and content monetization across all digital platforms.

What We Offer
  • Exclusive Music Catalog – 30+ artists, including the top 5 on Spotify.
  • Global Distribution – Through Believe Music Distribution & major platforms.
  • Strict Copyright Protection – Preventing fraudulent claims.
  • Cybersecurity & Content Management – Maximum security ensured.
  • Multi-Language Content – Songs in Bangla, Hindi, and Bhojpuri.
Our Reach

Operating worldwide across YouTube, Facebook, Spotify, and all major social media platforms.

Over 72,000+ listeners across our top 5 music categories. Partnering with labels, artists, and digital creators to revolutionize the Bangladeshi music industry.

Our Vision

At Chiklee Music, we are committed to building the best platform for all artists, ensuring they get the recognition and revenue they deserve.

We aim to dominate the digital music industry by continuously innovating and expanding our services.
